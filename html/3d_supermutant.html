League of Legends: Ahri Hiya guys! Here's a recreation of game character named "Ahri" from League of Legends. The photo was rendered in ZBrush, and polypainting was applied as texturing.
The 3D Character is completely game-ready, though animations (blendshapes etc.) were not made. The model is about 40 thousand polygons.
